About job.me

job.me is a technical interview readiness site, we aim to give users resource links and information to help ready them for technical interviews, we also present them with a small technical interview sim that allows them to answer questions in a multiple choice setting, while giving feedback.

upon recieving a final score users have that progress added to competency badges that are stored in the user panel. We encourage people to come test their knowledge and we look forward to helping you become technical interview ready!
